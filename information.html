Description and details of each game:

Rocket League:

Rocket League is a multiplayer based game where the player controls a single vehicle that has the ability to fly. Just like soccor, the main objective to hit the ball into the opponenets net. This game is considered very difficult because the player has to completely control the car from driving on the ground to hovering in mid air. For how difficult it is, learning new tricks and playstyles is very rewarding when playing Rocket League. More information can be found on their official website.

Overwatch:

Overwatch is a 6 versus 6 multiplayer game where players can choose different characters with unique abilities to complete objectives in the game. Most objectives are to either capture or defend a part of the map. With Overwatch, there are many different characters in the game, meaning that there is always at least one character that a player can be intrested in. More information can be found on their official website.

Clash Royale:

Clash Royale is a 1 on 1 strategic mobile game where the player defends his tower by placing cards that come to life. These cards that come to life then attack the enemy tower, where the enemy can also place these cards to defend their tower. The game has been grown increasingly because of its simple artstyle and unique complex gameplay. More information can be found on their official website.

https://clashroyale.com/

Apex Legeneds:

Apex Legends is a Battle royale where the player can choose different caracters with unique abilities (similar to Overwatch). A battle royale is when a large amount of players fignt on map until there is only one winner or one player standing (like the Hunger Games) They are very difficult because there are many opponents but it is very rewarding when winning a game out of a large amount of people. There are battle royales that can be as small as 10 players but can add to over 150 people. However, Apex Legends has a max of 60 players per game. More information can be found on their official website.

Beat Saber:

Beat Saber is a rhythm game that is played on a VR (Virtual Reality) Headset where you have two lightsabers. The main and only objective is to follow the rhythm by slashing large cubes in different directions at the right time. This game becomes very addicting once players are more experienced because harder levels become faster, with more movement and activeness. Whats unique about this game is how it is equivalent to working out some parts of the upper body if played regularly. More information can be found on their official website.

Echo Vr:

Echo VR is another virtual reality game where the players objective is to throw a frisbee into the opponents net. In this game, the player is able to float, glide, and propell themselves with rockets on them to grab the frisbee and get as many goals before the timer ends. This game is quite difficult (like Rocket League) but it is just as rewarding with enough practive. More information can be found on their official website.

Malody:

Malody is another rhythm game that reqires fast movement and high eye cordination to hit each note. Its similar to playing the piano but with less fingers to use with the price of a much higher speed. It also takes a lot of practive to even understand the game but it is very rewarding and fun to play. More information can be found on their official website.
